About Us

Supreme Info Consult – Ghana (SICG) is committed to enhancing the skills and impact of information professionals, including librarians, archivists, systems analysts, database administrators, and IT experts. We also provide basic IT training programs for tertiary and senior high school students.

Our Mission

To empower individuals and institutions with cutting-edge knowledge and professional skills in information management, digital literacy, and technology through consultancy and training services.

Our Vision

To become a leading information consultancy and training hub in Ghana and across Africa, driving innovation and excellence in the information sector.
